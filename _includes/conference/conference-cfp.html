GrafanaCon LA 2019 Announced!

Call for proposals now open.

GrafanaCon is a two-day event with talks centered around Grafana and the surrounding ecosystem. We are excited to announce the next GrafanaCon will be heading to Los Angeles, California, February 25-26, 2019!

Have a talk that’s waiting to be shared? A big idea you’d love to talk about? We’re looking for 30 minute detailed sessions, 20 minute general sessions, and 10 minute lightning talks. Come and share how you’re able to analyze and gain insights from your metrics, both with Grafana and the larger open source ecosystem. Technical and non-technical talks are welcome. Speakers receive free admission to the conference.

We are extending the deadline to submit your talk. We plan to close the window October 19, 2018 and notify selected speakers the first week of November. Speakers receive 1 free ticket to the conference. We will accept multiple proposals from each person.

All fields with an (*) asterisk are required. We don't require your Twitter or GitHub handles, but we'd love to include them on your badge.
